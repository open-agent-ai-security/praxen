Methodology how these are scored

Stars / cadence: gh api repos/<owner>/<repo> and .../stats/participation, snapshot 2026-07-11. Freshness (0–5): blended from last-push recency and sustained cadence, capped for archived/dormant repos — 5 pushed ≤1wk with heavy cadence · 4 ≤1mo clearly active · 3 ≤2mo maintained · 2 ≤3–4mo or sporadic · 1 archived / >4mo near-zero · 0.5 ~10mo+ abandoned. Maturity: the frozen median-of-3 raise_posture.weighted_overall from tests/baselines/v1.0.2-claude48/. Full per-target rationale and the retired targets' record live in tests/baselines/ROSTER_HEALTH.md. For the security dimensions, see the OWASP and RAISE coverage pages.
